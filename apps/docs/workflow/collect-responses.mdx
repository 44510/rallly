From the poll page, click the **Share** button to open the Invite dialog.

<Frame>![Share Poll](/images/meeting-poll/sharing.png)</Frame>

The dialog will show an invite link that allows anyone with the link to view your poll as a participant.
Keep the link safe and only share it with people you want to participate in your poll.

<Tip>Copy the link to your clipboard by clicking on it</Tip>
